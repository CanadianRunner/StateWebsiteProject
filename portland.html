About:

Portland is located in the North Western corner of the state. The citizens of Portland experience cool and rainy winters and warm and dry summers. Explore the city of Portland by visiting the Willamette and Columbia Rivers. Or catch a sports game at one of the metros 70+ active breweries. Learn about the history of Portland and the resources available to you.

Portland Quick Facts:

  1. Portland has a population of 652,503 and a metro population of 3,280,736
  2. It was incorporated in 1845
  3. It is located in the northwest region of the state
  4. It is an urban city, surronded by nature
  5. The median income is $50,271, compared to $71,562 for the state
