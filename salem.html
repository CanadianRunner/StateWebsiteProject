About:

Salem is located just 47 miles south of Portland and is also in the North Western corner of the state. The citizens of Salem experience cool and rainy winters and warm and dry summers. Explore the city of Salem by visiting the Capitol building and grounds. Or catch the state fair and cherry festival, as Salem is nicknamed "Cherry City". Learn about the history of Salem and the resources available to you.

Salem Quick Facts:
