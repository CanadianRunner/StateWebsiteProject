About:

Oregon is located in the Pacific Northwest of the United States. It is home to 4.2 million Oregonians and is the 27th most populous state. Oregon boasts over 98,000 square miles and is the ninth largest state by landmass. Explore the great state of Oregon by visiting the cities of Portland, Salem, Eugene. Learn about the history of Oregon and the resources available to you.

Oregon Quick Facts:

  • Highest elevation: 11,249ft
  • Admitted to Union: 1859 (33rd state)
  • It is located in the Pacific Northwest
  • It is bordered by Washington, Idaho, Nevada, and California
  • The median household income is $71,562
