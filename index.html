About:

Oregon is located in the Pacific Northwest of the United States. It is home to 4.2 million Oregonians and is the 27th most populous state. Oregon boasts over 98,000 square miles and is the ninth largest state by landmass.

Explore the great state of Oregon by visiting the cities of Portland, Salem, Eugene. If you're feeling adventerous stop by Crater Lake National park (pictured above). Learn about the history of Oregon and the resources available to you.
